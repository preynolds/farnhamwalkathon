Once Upon A Walkathon

“Once Upon A Walkathon” is Farnham’s biggest fundraiser of the year!  Not only do we raise lots of $$ for our school, but we have a ton fun at the walkathon, carnival, and silent auction.  Last year’s event raised over $95,000 that went directly back to our children and our schools.  With the funds raised last year, we were able to contribute towards the new outdoor learning spaces, new library books, field trips, stipends for our teachers, and much more.  This year, we will continue to help fund our important programs and technology, but also work towards raising money for shade structures on our playgrounds.  The Walkathon is held during the school day, with the carnival and silent auction held after school until 7:30 pm!  Come join us for a full day of Farnham Fun!
